# Paul Larche - Professional History & Context

## Career Milestones
- **Larche Communications Inc. (LCI):** Founded by Paul with the MOCHA philosophy ("Make Our Customers Happy Always").
- **Radio Portfolio:**
  - **KICX 99.5 (Kitchener):** Launched 2001, sold to Rogers in 2008.
  - **KICX & The Dock Brands:** Operated top-rated stations in Midland, Orillia, Sudbury, and Owen Sound.
  - **Awards:** KICX 106 was named CCMA Station of the Year 8 times.
  - **Bell Media Acquisition:** Remaining radio assets sold to Bell Media in 2018.
- **Agency Evolution:**
  - **Larche Digital (2015):** Launched to serve radio clients.
  - **Larche Branding/Marketing Agency (2018):** Full-service agency helping North American clients build emotionally resonant brands.
  - **Tyger Shark Acquisition (2019):** Expanded reach and capabilities.
  - **Treefrog Acquisition (2025):** The agency division was sold to Treefrog in Feb 2025.

## Book Context: The Divided Brain
- Paul's background in broadcasting and agency work directly informs the "Old Brain vs. New Brain" framework.
- The transition from radio (on-air connections) to digital/branding (emotional resonance) provides the real-world evidence for behavioral psychology in business.
